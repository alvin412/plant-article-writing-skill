# Systematic Review and Search

## Search Record

Record the question, concepts and aliases, species and crop names, traits, stresses, tissues, methods, databases, exact search strings, dates, restrictions, filters, inclusion and exclusion criteria, and update-search date. Search multiple appropriate sources and deduplicate by DOI, then normalized title and first author.

## Screening

Maintain counts and reasons for identified, deduplicated, title or abstract screened, full text assessed, included, excluded, and unavailable-full-text records. Prepare a PRISMA-style flow record for systematic reviews. Record a transparent search basis for narrative reviews as well.

## Appraisal and Synthesis

Appraise study design, biological replication, plant-system match, genetic or biochemical validation, environmental and developmental context, statistical adequacy, independent replication, and field or cross-species validation.

Synthesize by mechanism, scale, method, evidence strength, disagreement, or translation route. For meta-analysis, define effect sizes and analytical decisions before extracting or calculating results. Never reconstruct or fabricate effect sizes from incomplete reports.

Trigger an evidence-rescue search when a major claim lacks support, only one source supports a broad statement, a recent field is represented only by old sources, contexts do not match, contradictory evidence is absent, or a review is cited where primary evidence is required.
