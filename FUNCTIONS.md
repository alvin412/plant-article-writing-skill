# Function List / 功能列表

## English

### 1. Complete Plant Manuscript Writing

- Plans full publication-ready plant-science manuscripts.
- Produces a Paper Configuration Record covering paper type, target journal/style, language, citation format, species, tissue, stress/treatment, trait, pathway, data sources, and available figures/tables.
- Creates a detailed manuscript outline before drafting.
- Waits for user confirmation before writing the body of a new full manuscript.
- Drafts Abstract, Introduction, Results, Discussion, Methods, Conclusion, title, keywords, and figure legends.

### 2. Plant Review And Literature Review Writing

- Writes plant-science review articles, literature reviews, narrative reviews, and perspective-style reviews.
- Requires a detailed topic-specific outline before body drafting.
- Supports mechanism-centered, technology/resource, gene-family/pathway, crop-improvement, and multi-scale synthesis review structures.
- Groups literature by mechanism, evidence type, species, tissue, method, controversy, and unresolved questions instead of listing papers one by one.
- Proposes review figures, tables, boxes, future-perspective sections, and citation slots.

### 3. Bilingual Source-Paper Close Reading

- Reads source papers in a `nature-reader`-inspired style during writing.
- Produces bilingual close-reading notes with metadata, source anchors when available, original key claims, academic Chinese interpretation, plant context, evidence type, figure/table relevance, limitations, and citation-use slots.
- Preserves gene names, protein names, formulas, symbols, numerical values, species names, and technical terms.
- Uses source-grounded notes to support later manuscript claims.

### 4. Literature Foundation And Evidence Mapping

- Builds a literature matrix before drafting Introduction, Discussion, or review bodies.
- Maps each major manuscript claim to source evidence or user-provided results.
- Distinguishes evidence types such as GWAS/QTL/eQTL/TWAS, pangenome, transcriptome, single-cell/spatial omics, mutant, overexpression, knockout, complementation, biochemical assay, and field trial.
- Separates correlation, candidate-gene evidence, causal validation, and breeding implication.

### 5. Evidence Rescue For Introduction And Discussion

- Detects weakly supported claims in Introduction and Discussion.
- Triggers a `nature-academic-search`-style workflow when evidence is insufficient.
- Builds plant-specific search queries using organism names, gene/protein names, pathway terms, trait terms, stress/treatment terms, methods, and abbreviations.
- Follows multi-source search, deduplication, ranking, DOI/metadata verification, close reading, and citation-slot assignment before adding citations.
- Avoids adding unverified citations to final prose.

### 6. Section-Level Plant Writing

- Writes plant-science sections from claim to evidence to boundary to significance.
- Uses plant-specific argument structures for natural variation, genome/pangenome resources, single-cell/spatial atlases, immune or hormone crosstalk, developmental or transcription-factor modules, and multi-omics gene discovery.
- Keeps Results observation-led and Discussion interpretation-led.
- Produces publication-ready English plus formal academic Chinese when appropriate.

### 7. Manuscript Revision

- Diagnoses paragraph logic, claim support, evidence boundaries, and reviewer-facing risks.
- Revises existing drafts without inventing unsupported results or references.
- Classifies reviewer comments as major, minor, editorial, or citation-related.
- Revises only affected sections unless the user asks for a full rewrite.
- Keeps claim strength matched to validation depth.

### 8. Citation Checking

- Checks in-text citations against the reference list.
- Flags missing references, uncited references, metadata mismatches, weak support, outdated non-seminal sources, and references needing manual confirmation.
- Verifies DOI/PMID/title metadata when possible.
- Ensures review claims have sufficient source support or explicit evidence boundaries.

### 9. Plant-Specific Language And Claim Calibration

- Uses precise plant-science verbs such as identify, reveal, dissect, prioritize, validate, regulate, modulate, coordinate, mediate, confer, attenuate, profile, resolve, and integrate.
- Preserves gene/protein typography and scientific notation such as Na+, K+, H2O2, ROS, P values, and qPCR notation.
- Avoids overclaiming with terms such as prove, conclusively, unprecedented, best, unsupported first, and master regulator without sufficient evidence.
- Distinguishes association, prioritization, and causality.

### 10. Final QA

- Checks section purpose, claim support, citation integrity, figure/table references, plant terminology, species/tissue/stress boundaries, and bilingual consistency.
- Confirms that review sections synthesize arguments rather than list papers.
- Confirms that original research sections do not mix Results and Discussion logic.

## 中文

### 1. 完整植物学术论文写作

- 规划完整的发表级植物科学论文。
- 生成 Paper Configuration Record，记录论文类型、目标期刊或写作风格、语言、引用格式、物种、组织、胁迫或处理、性状、通路、数据来源以及已有图表。
- 在正式写正文前，先生成详细论文大纲。
- 新写完整论文时，必须等待用户确认大纲后再开始正文撰写。
- 支持撰写 Abstract、Introduction、Results、Discussion、Methods、Conclusion、title、keywords 和 figure legends。

### 2. 植物综述与文献综述写作

- 撰写植物科学综述、文献综述、叙述性综述和 perspective-style review。
- 正文撰写前必须先生成针对主题的详细大纲。
- 支持机制型综述、技术/资源型综述、基因家族/通路型综述、作物改良型综述和多尺度综合型综述。
- 按机制、证据类型、物种、组织、方法、争议和未解决问题组织文献，而不是逐篇罗列。
- 可设计综述中的概念图、表格、box、future perspectives 和 citation slots。

### 3. 文献中英对照精读

- 写作过程中参考 `nature-reader` 的逻辑阅读源文献。
- 生成中英对照精读笔记，包括 metadata、source anchors、英文核心观点、学术中文解读、植物背景、证据类型、图表相关性、局限性和 citation-use slots。
- 保留基因名、蛋白名、公式、符号、数值、物种名和专业术语。
- 使用有来源锚点的精读笔记支撑后续论文论点。

### 4. 文献基础与证据映射

- 在撰写 Introduction、Discussion 或综述正文前建立文献矩阵。
- 将每个主要论点映射到具体文献证据或用户提供的结果。
- 区分 GWAS/QTL/eQTL/TWAS、泛基因组、转录组、单细胞/空间组学、突变体、过表达、敲除、互补、 biochemical assay 和田间试验等证据类型。
- 区分相关性证据、候选基因证据、因果验证和育种应用推断。

### 5. 引言和讨论的证据补强

- 检测 Introduction 和 Discussion 中支撑不足的论点。
- 当证据不足时，触发类似 `nature-academic-search` 的补充检索流程。
- 根据物种名、基因/蛋白名、通路术语、性状术语、胁迫/处理术语、方法和缩写构建植物领域检索式。
- 在加入引用前，执行多源检索、去重、排序、DOI/题录核验、精读和 citation-slot 分配。
- 不把未核验文献直接加入最终正文。

### 6. 植物论文分章节写作

- 按“论点 -> 证据 -> 边界 -> 意义”的逻辑撰写植物科学章节。
- 支持自然变异、基因组/泛基因组资源、单细胞/空间图谱、免疫或激素串扰、发育或转录因子模块、多组学基因挖掘等植物类文章结构。
- 保持 Results 以观察和结果为主，Discussion 以解释和意义为主。
- 可输出发表级英文，并在需要时提供正式学术中文版本。

### 7. 稿件修改

- 诊断段落逻辑、论点支撑、证据边界和审稿风险。
- 修改已有草稿，同时避免编造不存在的结果或参考文献。
- 将审稿意见归类为 major、minor、editorial 或 citation-related。
- 除非用户要求全文重写，否则只修改受影响的部分。
- 根据验证深度校准论点强度。

### 8. 引用检查

- 检查正文引用和参考文献列表是否匹配。
- 标记缺失参考文献、未引用参考文献、题录不一致、支撑不足、过旧且非经典文献，以及需要人工确认的文献。
- 在可能时核验 DOI、PMID、标题、期刊和年份。
- 确保综述中的重要论点有足够文献支撑，或明确写出证据边界。

### 9. 植物领域语言与论点强度校准

- 使用准确的植物科学动词，例如 identify、reveal、dissect、prioritize、validate、regulate、modulate、coordinate、mediate、confer、attenuate、profile、resolve 和 integrate。
- 保留基因/蛋白书写规范，以及 Na+、K+、H2O2、ROS、P 值和 qPCR 表达式等科学记号。
- 避免使用 prove、conclusively、unprecedented、best、缺乏支撑的 first，以及证据不足时的 master regulator。
- 明确区分关联、候选基因优先级和因果关系。

### 10. 最终质量检查

- 检查章节任务、论点支撑、引用完整性、图表引用、植物术语、物种/组织/胁迫边界和中英文一致性。
- 确认综述章节是在综合论证，而不是逐篇罗列文献。
- 确认原创研究论文没有把 Results 和 Discussion 的写法混在一起。
